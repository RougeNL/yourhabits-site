Privacy Policy for YourHabits

Effective date: May 5, 2026

YourHabits is a native iPhone habit tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what data is used, why it is used, and what choices you have.

1. Data you create in the app

YourHabits may store habit-related data on your device, including habit names, categories, colors, icons, completion history, streaks, progress data, timer sessions, reminder settings, and app preferences such as theme and layout options.

This data is used only to provide and improve app features for you.

2. Local storage and iCloud status

Your habit data is primarily stored locally on your device. YourHabits does not require an account for core habit tracking.

YourHabits does not currently use iCloud sync for habit data. If this changes, this policy will be updated before or when that feature is released.

3. Notifications

If you enable reminders, YourHabits schedules local notifications on your device. Notification permissions are managed by iOS and can be changed in iOS Settings.

Reminder delivery may be affected by device settings, Focus modes, battery settings, or iOS behavior.

4. Apple Health permissions

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, YourHabits may request permission to read or write selected Health data types.

YourHabits accesses Health data only after permission is granted, and only for user-selected habit and progress functionality. YourHabits does not use Health data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

You can revoke Health permissions at any time in the Health app or iOS Settings.

5. Purchases, subscriptions, and RevenueCat

YourHabits may offer optional paid upgrades (YourHabits Plus) through App Store purchases or subscriptions. Purchases are processed by Apple.

YourHabits uses RevenueCat to manage purchase status, subscriptions, entitlements, and restore purchases.

RevenueCat may process purchase-related data (for example subscription status and transaction metadata) for subscription functionality and service analytics. YourHabits does not receive or store full payment card details.

6. Third-party services

YourHabits may use the following third-party service:

RevenueCat
Purpose: managing in-app purchases, subscriptions, entitlement access, and purchase restoration.
Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
Terms: https://www.revenuecat.com/terms

7. Advertising, sale of data, and tracking

YourHabits does not use third-party advertising.

YourHabits does not sell personal data.

YourHabits does not track users across third-party apps or websites.

8. Data retention and deletion

You can edit or delete habits and entries inside the app at any time.

Deleting the app may delete locally stored app data, subject to normal iOS behavior and backups.

Purchase-related records managed by Apple or RevenueCat may be retained as required for transaction history, fraud prevention, accounting, legal compliance, or support purposes.

9. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data processing.

Because core habit data is primarily stored on your device, you can usually exercise many controls directly in the app and iOS settings. For purchase-related data managed by Apple or RevenueCat, requests may need to be made through those providers.

10. Children's privacy

YourHabits is not specifically directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this website.
