Privacy Policy for YourHabits

Effective date: 01-June-2026

YourHabits is a habit tracking app designed to help users create, track, and review personal habits.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and what choices users have.

1. Data stored in the app

YourHabits may store habit-related data on your device, including:

  • Habit names
  • Habit categories
  • Habit colors and icons
  • Completion history
  • Streaks and progress data
  • Timer sessions
  • Reminder settings
  • App preferences, such as themes and layout settings

This data is used only to provide the core habit tracking features of the app.

2. Local data storage

Your habit data is primarily stored locally on your device. YourHabits does not require an account to use the core habit tracking features.

3. Notifications

If you enable reminders, YourHabits may schedule local notifications on your device. Notification permissions are controlled by iOS. You can change or revoke notification access at any time in your device settings.

4. Apple Health

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, YourHabits may request permission to read from or write to selected Apple Health data types.

YourHabits only accesses Apple Health data after you give permission through the iOS Health permission screen.

Apple Health data is used only for features directly related to habit tracking and personal progress. YourHabits does not use Apple Health data for advertising, marketing, tracking, or data mining.

You can change or revoke Apple Health permissions at any time in the Health app or iOS Settings.

5. Purchases and subscriptions

YourHabits may offer optional premium features through in-app purchases or subscriptions. Purchases are processed by Apple.

YourHabits uses RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service, to manage purchase status, subscriptions, entitlements, and restore purchases.

RevenueCat may process purchase-related information, such as purchase history and subscription status, for app functionality and analytics related to purchases.

YourHabits does not receive or store your full payment card details.

6. Third-party services

YourHabits may use the following third-party service:

RevenueCat
Purpose: Managing in-app purchases, subscriptions, premium access, and purchase restoration.
RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

7. Tracking and advertising

YourHabits does not use third-party advertising.

YourHabits does not sell your personal data.

YourHabits does not use your habit data or Apple Health data for advertising or tracking across apps and websites.

8. iCloud sync

YourHabits does not currently use iCloud sync for habit data.

If iCloud sync is added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what data is synced and how it is handled.

9. Data deletion

You can delete habit data directly in the app by deleting habits or entries.

You can also delete the app from your device to remove locally stored app data, subject to normal iOS behavior and backups.

For purchase-related records managed by Apple or RevenueCat, some information may be retained as required for transaction history, fraud prevention, accounting, or legal compliance.

10. User choices

You can control:

  • Notification permissions in iOS Settings
  • Apple Health permissions in the Health app or iOS Settings
  • Subscription management through your Apple ID subscription settings
  • Habit data by editing or deleting habits in the app

11. Children’s privacy

YourHabits is not specifically directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be made available through the app or the App Store listing.
